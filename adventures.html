Adventures

Ross Sea, Antarctica

I have been on two research cruises (54 days in 2013, 78 days in 2018) to the Ross Sea, Antarctica. The Ross Sea is considered to be the most pristine marine ecosystem on Earth and is one of the most species-rich areas of the Southern Ocean.

Chagos Archipelago, Indian Ocean

I have been on two research cruises (16 days in 2016, 20 days in 2018) to the Chagos Archipelago, located in the middle of the Indian Ocean. The archipelago consists of 55 tiny islands spread across 640,000 km2 of ocean.

Diving Sudan

Sudan has the best diving in the Red Sea. Hammerhead sharks are seen on 40% of dives, including in large schools. They are found at 50 meters depth at the edge of plateaus where the current splits into two, providing cooler oxygenated water.
